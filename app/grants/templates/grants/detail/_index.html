What happens next?

The Gitcoin team reviews each grant before it goes live on the website. Grants are reviewed more regularly in periods around active grant rounds. A list of all the grants, their approval stage, and comments from reviewers can be found here: Public Grant Approval Oversight

The Anti-Fraud & Collusion workstream of GitcoinDAO has a sub-stream called Grant Verifications. The community members & stewards review each grant, then approve or deny it based on the policy outlined below.

For more information, visit Grants Round FAQs or Gitcoin Discord
